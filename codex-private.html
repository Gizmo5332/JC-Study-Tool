About
Arché · Codex
Your personal code literacy school — built around your own apps, using the Ebbinghaus spaced repetition method to lock in knowledge over time.
How It Works

1. Read — Each lesson explains a concept in detail, with real code examples from your own apps.

2. Quiz — Pass at 70% to mark a lesson complete. Three attempts before a cooldown.

3. Rate Confidence — 1–5 stars after passing. Your rating sets the next review date.

4. Review — The Ebbinghaus scheduler brings concepts back at the right intervals so they stick.
